Napoleon Bonaparte

“Emperor of the French”


Here is a time line of Napoleon Bonaparte's life:

Early years

  • 1769 - Born in Ajaccio, Corsica.
  • 1785 - After completing his education in a military school in France, Napoleon joins the French military, becoming an artillery officer
  • 1787 - The French Revolution begins, and Napoleon sides with the revolutionaries. He fights in the French Revolutionary Wars in Toulon and Paris.
  • 1793 - Napoleon is promoted to Brigadier General.
  • 1795 - A new constitution in France places executive power in a five-member Directory. Napoleon becomes a respected advisor on military matters to the Directory.
  • 1796 - Napoleon marries Joséphine Tascher de La Pagerie, the widow of General Alexandre de Beauharnais, who was guillotined during the Reign of Terror.
  • 1797 - Napoleon leads French troops against Austria in the First War of Coalition to a series of victories in Northern Italy. He makes peace with the Austrians in Treaty of Campo Formio.
  • 1798 - Napoleon is sent to Egypt to undermine British access to India, but is defeated by Admiral Horatio Nelson in Battle of the Nile. The French geodesists helped to find the Rosetta Stone.


    • Napoleonic Era

  • 1799 - Napoleon recieves news of turmoil in France and performs a coup d'état and overthrows the Directory. A Consulate is formed as new government, with Napoleon as the First Consul.
  • 1800 - Napoleon faces the Austrian army by crossing his entire army across the Alps and defeats them in Battle of Marengo. Later in the year escapes an assassination attempt.
  • 1803 - In need for money, Napoleon sells the French colony of Louisiana Territory to the United States in North America.
  • 1804 - Napoleon introduces the Napoleonic Code, a Frech civil code that is a collection of laws across the France. Napoleon them proclaims himself as the Emperor of France.
  • 1805 - Napoleon faces off against the coalition for the third time and wins a decisive battle at Austerlitz against Austrian and Prussian forces. Napoleon's fleet is wrecked by Admiral Nelson in Battle of Trafalgar, who was killed in the battle.
  • 1806 - Napoleon makes peace with the Austrian Emperor with a harsh treaty, declares himself as the Protector of the Rhine. King Francis II, dissolves the Holy Roman Empire after this treaty. Napoleon makes his brother Joseph King of Naples.
  • 1807 - Wins Battle of Eylau, Battle of Friedland. Russia makes peace with France.
  • 1809 - Napoleon faces his first defeat in Battle of Aspern, and divorces his wife Joséphine.
  • 1810 - Napoleon marries Marie Louise, Duchess of Parma, Austria in Paris.
  • 1811 - Napoleon's son, Napoleon Francis Joseph Charles is born.
  • 1812 - Napoleon invades Russia and reaches until Moscow only to find the city evacuated. He retreats to France with skirmishes and battles along the route.
  • 1813-14 - Napoleon fights a series of battle s that ultimately results in him abdicating the throne and going to exile in Elba with his family, while the Bourbon family is restored to power with Louis XVIII as king.


    • Last years

  • 1815 - Napoleon escapes from Elba and retakes Paris, won in Battle of Ligny, lost in Battle of Waterloo to Duke of Wellington and coalition of English and a Prussian army. Napoleon is exiled to Saint Helena and Louis XVIII is restored.
  • 1815-21 - Napoleon dictates and publishes his memoirs while in exile. He wrote a book about Julius Caesar, and learnt English enough to read English newspapers.
  • 1821 - Napoleon dies in Saint Helena.

“Death is nothing, but to live defeated and inglorious is to die daily.”

-- Napoléon Bonaparte.
